Furniture Shop - Security Policy

Security Policy

Your security is our top priority. We have implemented several measures to ensure your personal and payment information is kept safe:

1. Data Encryption

We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption to protect sensitive data, such as your payment and personal details, while being transmitted over the internet.

2. Secure Payment Methods

We offer secure payment gateways that comply with PCI DSS standards, ensuring your financial transactions are encrypted and safe from fraud.

3. Privacy Protection

We respect your privacy. Your information will never be shared with third parties without your consent. We follow strict privacy policies to protect your personal data.

4. Regular Security Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability tests to ensure that our systems are secure and your data remains protected.

5. Account Protection

We encourage our users to create strong passwords for their accounts. Additionally, we offer two-factor authentication (2FA) for enhanced security.
