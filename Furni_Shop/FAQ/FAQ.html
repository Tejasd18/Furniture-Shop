Furniture Shop - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Simply browse through our products, select the items you wish to buy, and click on "Add to Cart". Once you're ready, proceed to checkout to complete your order.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. For more details, check our Payment Methods page.

3. How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the items you ordered. Typically, orders are delivered within 5-7 business days.

4. Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be canceled or changed within 24 hours of purchase. After that, please refer to our Cancellation Policy for more details.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping fees and delivery times will vary depending on your location.
