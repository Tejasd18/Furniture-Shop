Cancellation Policy

We want to make your shopping experience as smooth as possible. Here’s our cancellation policy:

1. Cancellation Before Shipping

If you cancel your order before it’s shipped, you will receive a full refund to your original payment method.

2. Cancellation After Shipping

If your order has already been shipped, you can still cancel it by contacting our customer support, but you may be responsible for return shipping fees.

3. Non-Cancellable Items

Some items, like custom or made-to-order furniture, cannot be cancelled once the order is placed. Check the product description for more information.

4. How to Cancel

To cancel your order, please contact our customer support team at support@furnitureshop.com or call us at (123) 456-7890.
